The FCDO design system

The FCDO is introducing a new design system aimed at improving the digital experience for users of government services. This system is specifically designed to ensure consistency, accessibility, and usability across FCDO's digital platforms, making it easier for citizens, businesses, and government employees to interact with FCDOâ€™s online services.

Why a new design system?

The introduction of the FCDO design system reflects the department's commitment to creating high-quality, user-friendly, and accessible digital services for all. By improving efficiency across teams, reducing duplication of effort, and ensuring consistency, the system should allow the department to deliver better services in less time and at a lower cost.

As the digital landscape evolves, the design system will be continuously updated to incorporate best practices, feedback from users, and new technologies. Ultimately, the goal is to provide citizens and users with a seamless, efficient, and engaging experience when interacting with the FCDO online.

Benefits of a design system

Consistency

  • Ensures a uniform look and feel across all platforms and touchpoints
  • Reduces design and development inconsistencies
  • Creates a shared language between designers and developers
  • Enhances brand recognition and trust

Efficiency & Speed

  • Saves time by providing reusable components and patterns
  • Helps teams prototype and build products faster
  • Reduces redundant design and development efforts
  • Streamlines onboarding for new team members

Scalability

  • Makes it easier to expand a product without redesigning from scratch
  • Supports multiple teams working on different aspects of the same ecosystem
  • Ensures a seamless experience as products grow in complexity
  • Facilitates cross-team collaboration and alignment

Accessibility & Inclusivity

  • Encourages WCAG compliance and better usability for diverse audiences
  • Standardizes contrast, readability, and keyboard navigation
  • Improves user experience for people with disabilities
  • Fosters a culture of inclusive design

Maintainability & Governance

  • Provides a single source of truth for design and development
  • Ensures updates are systematically applied across all products
  • Reduces technical debt and design inconsistencies
  • Encourages contributions and improvements from the entire team
